About Raisins

A Raisin is a dry grape.Raisins are produced in many regions of the world and may be eaten raw or used in cooking , brewing and baking. Raisins can vary in different sizes, shapes , colors and taste, depending on the type of grapes and method of process.

Raisins are packed with energy and rich in fibres, vitamins and minerals. They are naturally sweet ,high in sugar and calories ,beneficial to health when eaten in moderation.Basically, raisins are dark coloured or light coloured. Dark coloured raisins are sun dried ,typically by laying them out in vineyards for 2-4 weeks ,after which raisins are cleaned , graded and packed. Light coloured raisins are treated in controlled conditions to preserve their colour.

Raisins need to be stores in cool dry place. If properly stored, they can last for years. That is why , raisins are stored in cold storages.
