Black Raisins

Black Raisins also known as 'Sharad' in India are dark black naturally sun dried raisins and hence very sweet in taste.

Black Raisins can be made from any type of grape by allowing them to sun dry and hence gets almost dark black in color. These are free from chemicals and any processing, making them extremely sweet and delicious. Because of these characteristics, they are in high demand in export market.

Black seedless raisins are loaded with juiciness and nutritive value. They are free from residue and artificial sweeteners.They are fat free and cholestrol free. It is also great choice to increase haemoglobin levels making it a healthy snack.
