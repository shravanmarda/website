About Us

Our firm M/s Satyanarayan Gulabchand Marda,established way back in 1955 by our grandfather Lt.Shri Gulabchandji Marda. have been suppliers of various commodities depending on the arrivals of those in Sangli Market. Over the period of time, these commodity arrivals have changed and so is our business. Started off with Tobacco, Groundnut seeds, later got into supply of Shikakai and Jaggery. Today , since Sangli is one of the big producer of high quality turmeric and also the epicenter of Raisins (Dry grapes or say 'Kishmish') producing are in India, we are currently focused on dealing and supplying these products within and outside India,as per the customer needs with customer satisfaction being the ultimate motive. Though products might changing over the period, principles like Quality, Trust & Satisfaction have been throughout strong pioneers of our business.
